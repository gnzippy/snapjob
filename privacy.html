Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

SnapJob ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our job management platform.

1. Information we collect

  • Business name, contact number, and email address provided during signup
  • Job details including customer names, phone numbers, and addresses entered by the business owner
  • Invoice data including services rendered and payment amounts
  • Technician names and phone numbers added by the business owner
  • WhatsApp message content when customers contact your business number
  • Uploaded files including business logo and PayNow QR code

2. How we use your information

  • To provide and operate the SnapJob job management dashboard
  • To send WhatsApp notifications to technicians when jobs are assigned
  • To generate invoices with your business details and PayNow QR
  • To store your job history and customer records
  • To improve and develop the SnapJob platform

3. WhatsApp and messaging

SnapJob uses the Meta WhatsApp Business API to send job notifications to technicians. By using SnapJob, you confirm that you have obtained appropriate consent from your technicians to receive WhatsApp messages through our platform. We do not use customer or technician phone numbers for any purpose other than job-related notifications.

4. Data storage

Your data is stored securely using Supabase (hosted on AWS ap-northeast-1, Singapore region). Uploaded files including logos and PayNow QR codes are stored in Supabase Storage. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

5. Data sharing

We do not sell your data to third parties. We share data only with:

  • Supabase — for database and file storage
  • Meta (WhatsApp Business API) — for sending job notifications
  • Vercel — for hosting the platform

6. Data retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. You may request deletion of your account and associated data by contacting us at hello@snapjob.sg. Job records and invoices may be retained for up to 7 years for accounting and legal compliance purposes.

7. Your rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at hello@snapjob.sg. We will respond within 30 days.

8. Cookies

SnapJob uses session cookies to keep you logged in. We do not use tracking or advertising cookies.

9. Children's privacy

SnapJob is intended for business use only and is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify registered users of significant changes via email or WhatsApp.

11. Contact us

For any privacy-related questions or requests:
